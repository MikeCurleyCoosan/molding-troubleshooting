Stringing

Stringing is usually due to the gates not freezing properly before the mold opens.

Process improvements

1. Reduce mold and hot runner temperatures - This will reduce the melt temperature and facilitate the freeze of the gate

2. Reduce back pressure and RPM - Again will reduce the melt density (temp) and facilitate the freeze of the gate

3. Increase pullback - This will reduce the melt pressure, improving drooling

4. Increase holding time and cooling time - This increases the time for the gates to freeze (If cycletime and reach maximum as per I0IDP-0122 -Increase Plasticising delay)

5. Increase Mold Open Speed - This may help break the stringing

6. If all above fail, time to consider changing the A-half mold base
