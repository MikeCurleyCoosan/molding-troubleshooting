Base Curve Parts Sticking to A-side Troubleshooting

The following steps should be followed if you encounter parts sticking to the Base Curve A-side plates

Parts sticking to the BC A side mold plates is usually due to parts being underpacked.

Execute the following step in exclusively the prescribed order

Clean Cavity (Have plates returned to toolroom for this)

Increase Pack Pressure 2

>

Increase Pack Pressure 1

Rotate B side plate. If issue follows related to B-side plate, stays related to A-side

Decrease cooling time

Decrease holding time 2 (Holding time 1 & 2 are normally set to maximum

Burn tips - inspeck tips/Mold base

Escalate to METL/PTL, Molding SME

]
