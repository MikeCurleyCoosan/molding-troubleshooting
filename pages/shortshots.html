Short shots - Mold is not completely filled

Short shots are usually due to one of the three reasons listed below. Follow the troubleshooting proceess which best matches the issue you are experiencing.

Plastic cools before it gets across the mold - Melt temperature is too low.

1. Increase Mold temperature/Barrel temperature

2. Check individual hot runner temperatures

3. Increase RPM/Injection Speed/Back pressure

Not enough material is injected into the cavity - Increase cavity fill

1. Increase shot size/Back pressure/RPM.

2. Increase hold pressure/hold time

3. Nozzle filter needs replacement. (If this is the case it will show in the filling injection pressure (too high))

4. Damaged check-ring. Test if the check-ring is leaking. Increase the hold time to 10seconds and monitor if the cushion stabilises (check-ring ok), or dissapears (check-ring damaged)

Blocked gates

1. Usually contamination in hot runner system

2. Increase temperature of effected hot runner tip.

3. Increase hold pressure to blast the gate
