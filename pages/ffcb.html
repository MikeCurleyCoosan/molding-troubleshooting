Front Curve Parts Sticking to B-side Troubleshooting

The following steps should be followed if you encounter parts sticking to the Front Curve B-side plates

Parts sticking to the FC B side mold plates is usually due to parts being underpacked.

Execute the following step in exclusively the prescribed order

1. Clean Cavity (Have plates returned to toolroom for this)

2. Adjust Yushin Kick position - out first (0.1-0.5mm)

3. Inspect Yushin cups and stems

4. Increase Pack Pressure 2

5. Increase Pack Pressure 1

6. Adjust Yushin Timers - Increase ejector delay. Increase Kick return (Cycletime will increase)

7. Incrase pack time to max (fully formed part)

8. Rotate B side plate. If issue follows related to B-side plate, stays related to A-side

9. Increase cooling time

10. Burn tips - inspeck tips/Mold base

11. Escalate to METL/PTL, Molding SME
